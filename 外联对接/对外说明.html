一句话定位

散户的交易复盘 + 情绪风控教练。

基于用户自己的操盘记录，用技术面模型 + memento caseBank，

告诉他「你这一单是不是又在追涨杀跌」，帮他管住手、少犯同样的错。

我们解决什么问题

散户亏钱，多数不是不懂行情，而是管不住自己的手——追涨杀跌、重复同一类错误。

市场上的产品都在「给信号、荐标的」，没人帮散户复盘自己的行为、约束自己的情绪。

我们怎么做

  • 交易复盘：导入用户自己的真实操盘记录，逐单回放。
  • 行为诊断：用技术面模型 + 案例库（memento caseBank），识别「这一单是不是又在追高/恐慌割肉」。
  • 情绪风控：在用户即将重复犯错时，做交易纪律提醒（「你在追高，冷静」）。
  • 情绪价值：陪伴式教练，而非冷冰冰的信号机。

合规边界（重要）

卟卟鸡不是荐股工具，不碰持牌业务。我们讲的是「用户自己」，不是荐证券：

  • ✅ 分析用户自己的历史操盘、做行为复盘
  • ✅ 情绪管理 / 交易纪律提醒（行为干预，非证券建议）
  • ✅ 通用投资者教育、风险知识、方法论
  • ⛔ 不给个股风险评级、不预测涨跌、不给买卖点/目标价

这条边界是产品设计的底层原则，从根上规避了荐股牌照风险。

BuBuJi · One-Pager (English)

One-pager for VC outreach. Keep in sync with 对外说明_CN.md whenever the product definition changes.
Last updated: 2026-06-04

One-liner

A trade-review and emotional risk-control coach for retail traders.

Built on a user's own trading records, combining a technical-analysis model with a

memento caseBank, it tells the user "are you chasing highs and panic-selling again?"

— helping them stay disciplined and stop repeating the same mistakes.

The Problem

Retail traders mostly don't lose money because they misread the market — they lose

because they can't control their own hands: chasing rallies, panic-selling,

repeating the same class of mistakes. Every product out there pushes signals and

stock picks; nobody helps the retail trader **review their own behavior and master

their own emotions**.

What We Do

  • Trade review — import the user's real trading records and replay each trade.
  • Behavioral diagnosis — a technical model + case bank (memento caseBank) flags

"is this trade chasing a high / panic-cutting again?"

  • Emotional risk control — discipline nudges right before the user repeats a

mistake ("you're chasing a high — cool down").

  • Emotional value — a companion-style coach, not a cold signal machine.

Compliance Boundary (Important)

BuBuJi is not a stock-picking tool and stays out of licensed territory. We talk

about the *user themselves*, not about recommending securities:

  • ✅ Analyze the user's own trading history; behavioral review
  • ✅ Emotion management / trading-discipline reminders (behavioral, not investment advice)
  • ✅ General investor education, risk knowledge, methodology
  • ⛔ No per-stock risk ratings, no price predictions, no entry/exit or target prices

This boundary is a foundational design principle that structurally avoids

stock-advisory licensing risk.
